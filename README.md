# betterSky

betterSky is a mobile weather app that lets you check current conditions and a multi-day forecast for your current location or any city you choose. You can save your favourite cities for quick access, compare across cities, and view historic data.

## Potential Features

- Use current location
- Check other locations
- Save Favorites
- Compare two places
- Historic data
- Similar city suggestions based on liked cities and weather patterns

## Similar City Suggestions

betterSky will learn from the cities and weather conditions you like most. When you favourite a city or consistently check places with similar climates, the app will suggest other cities with comparable temperature ranges, weather patterns, and seasonal conditions so you can discover new destinations that match your preferences.
